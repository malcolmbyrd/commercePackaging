About Us

Commerce Packaging has been family owned and operated since it was founded in 1954 by Stanley Alexander. Originally focused on serving the specialized packaging demand of military contractors, Commerce Packaging utilizes the same equipment and meticulous craftsmanship to service commercial and industrial customers as well. Commerce Packaging is built upon Stanley Alexanderâ€™s principles of providing the best service to customers.

We are here to satisfy our customers and have grown and evolved to their ever changing needs. Nonetheless, our focus remains grounded in the principles established nearly 60 years ago.

We differentiate ourselves by providing exceptional service allowing our customers to trust they are dealing with professionals and their product will be packaged safely and completed on time and on budget.

We have extensive experience complying with military specifications and guarantee your package will be in compliance with all regulations. We are also equipped to provide DD-250, WAWF and RFID service.

We are USDA- and NeLMA-certified for the manufacturing of heat-treated crates.
