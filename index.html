We Sell Service

Commerce Packaging is family owned and operated with nearly 60 years of experience providing packaging solutions to our customers.

We are a full-service packaging company and will work with you to create a safe, durable and cost-effective solution to your packaging needs and remain committed to providing premier, personalized customer service.

Since we commenced operations, we have supported our military with quality packaging our armed services can rely upon. We are well versed in military specification packaging standards and are equipped to process DD-250, WAWF and RFIDs.

Our warehouse stocks a comprehensive inventory of packaging materials, ensuring our ability to complete your order promptly. We will also work with you to streamline your logistics requirements.

Our modern 70,000-square-foot facility is conveniently located in Norwalk, CT.
